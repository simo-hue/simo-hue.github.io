## Introduction

Visiting Poland offers a unique opportunity to connect with history. The city of Kraków, with its beautiful architecture and vibrant culture, stands in stark contrast to the nearby Auschwitz Birkenau camp, which serves as a solemn and powerful reminder of the tragedies of the past.

## Auschwitz Birkenau

The visit to Auschwitz Birkenau is an essential, albeit heavy, experience. It provides a stark look at the realities of the Holocaust and ensures that the memory of those who suffered is never forgotten. Walking through the camps is a deeply moving experience that leaves a lasting impact.

## Kraków

In contrast to the heavy history of Auschwitz, the city of Kraków is full of life. Its historic center is beautifully preserved, offering wonderful sights, delicious food, and a glimpse into Polish culture and traditions. The juxtaposition of these two locations makes a trip to Poland both educational and unforgettable.
