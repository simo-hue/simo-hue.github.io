## Introduction

Traveling to Budapest with Collegio Don Mazza was an experience blending cultural discovery with great company. The city, divided by the mighty Danube River into Buda and Pest, offers two distinct atmospheres that we had the pleasure to explore together.

## Highlights of the Trip

From the historic Buda Castle to the magnificent Parliament Building on the Pest side, the architecture was simply stunning. We also took the time to relax in the famous thermal baths, a must-do when visiting the city. The trip wasn't just about sightseeing; it was about the shared experiences, the late-night conversations along the riverbank, and the memories forged with friends from Collegio Don Mazza.
